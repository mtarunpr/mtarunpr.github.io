Tarun Prasad

I’m a fourth-year undergraduate at Harvard studying computer science and mathematics. I’m fortunate to have Nada Amin as my advisor and to have previously been advised by Madhu Sudan. My research interests lie in the field of algorithms and complexity theory, especially in the context of approximation algorithms and constraint satisfaction problems.
